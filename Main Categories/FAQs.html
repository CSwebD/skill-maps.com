Frequently Asked Questions

How do I track my progress?

You can create an account on Skill Maps to unlock progress tracking across all roadmaps.

Are the learning pathways free?

Yes, all learning pathways and resources on Skill Maps are completely free and open to all learners.

Can I contribute content?

Definitely! Join our community via Discord or contact us to collaborate.

Are there certificates?

We are working on adding certificate features to some roadmaps and challenges.

Who maintains Skill Maps?

A team of software engineers and educators dedicated to open-source learning. Check the About page for more info.
